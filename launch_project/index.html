About

“True art is characterized by an irresistible urge in the creative artist.”

-Albert Einstein

✦✧✦✧✦

Hello! My name is Molly. I started this blog with the intention to help and inspire aspiring artists. In this blog I provide advice on how to deal with struggles all artists inevitably encounter. For instance, artist’s block, criticism, and how to learn from mistakes. Additionally, I recommend tips, techniques, and materials that have worked best for many artists including myself. Also, I talk about my personal experience as an artist, what I’ve learned along the way, and other things I’ve picked up that may be helpful to other aspiring artists.
